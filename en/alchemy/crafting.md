# Crafting — I want to craft

Crafting takes two minutes. Three steps:

## 1. Pick a recipe

Open a lab and choose a **live recipe**. The recipe card shows exactly:

* **You will receive** — the reward and how many crafts are still **available** in stock
* **Ingredients** — every input, its required amount per craft, and how much of it you already have in **escrow** and in your **wallet**

## 2. Craft in one click

Press **Craft**:

* Missing inputs move from your wallet into **escrow** automatically.
* The craft fires in a **single wallet approval** — no multi-step signing.
* A **discount NFT** (if you hold one) activates in the same batch, cutting the craft fee up to −100%.
* Escrow is used first; any excess fee is **refunded**.

## 3. Receive the reward

Rewards arrive **seconds later** — the drawer shows exactly what you got.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If a delivery ever fails (for example, you weren't registered on the reward token), the reward waits **safely** in **My items → Claimable**. Nothing is ever lost.
{% endhint %}

## My items

Your personal Lab inventory:

* **Escrow** — inputs parked under recipes; withdraw anytime
* **Claimable** — rewards pending pickup after a failed delivery
