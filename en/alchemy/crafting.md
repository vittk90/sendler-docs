# Crafting — I want to craft

Crafting takes two minutes. Here's how it works in plain terms.

## 1. Open a recipe

Every recipe is a simple trade: **what you give** and **what you get**. Open a live recipe and you'll see:

* **You will receive** — the reward, and how many crafts are still available
* **Ingredients** — everything the recipe asks for, and how much of it you currently have

## 2. See what you already have vs. what's missing

For each ingredient, Sendler shows two numbers:

* **escrow** — how much of that item you've already pre-deposited on the platform for this recipe
* **wallet** — how much you're holding in your regular NEAR wallet

You don't need to prepare anything in advance. If your escrow doesn't cover the recipe, Sendler simply takes the rest **directly from your wallet** the moment you craft.

## 3. Craft in one click

Press **Craft**:

* Anything missing moves from your wallet automatically — in the **same transaction**, with a single wallet approval.
* An **Adam discount NFT** (if you hold one) is applied automatically, cutting the craft fee based on its rarity.
* Any pre-deposited amount is used first; if you overpay the fee, the excess is **refunded**.

## 4. Receive the reward

Rewards arrive **seconds later** — you'll see exactly what you got.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If a delivery ever fails (for example, you weren't registered on the reward token), the reward waits **safely** in **My items → Claimable**. Nothing is ever lost — you can pick it up any time.
{% endhint %}

## My items

Your personal Lab inventory:

* **Escrow** — anything you've pre-deposited for a recipe; withdraw it anytime, even if you change your mind
* **Claimable** — rewards waiting to be picked up after a failed delivery
