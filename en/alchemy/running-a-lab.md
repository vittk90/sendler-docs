# Running a Lab — I run a lab

Turn your collection into a crafting economy. Four steps:

## 1. Create a lab

One on-chain transaction:

* **Name:** 1–128 characters, Latin only
* **Creation fee:** 5 Ⓝ — any excess is refunded
* The lab starts empty; you add recipes and fund the reward stock from the lab page

## 2. Build a recipe

The recipe constructor lets you define:

* **Inputs** — *required* items or flexible **"any one of"** groups
* **Group entire NFT collections into a single ingredient** — accept any of thousands of tokens as valid input
* **The reward** — what a successful craft delivers

## 3. Fund the reward stock

Transfer the reward FTs/NFTs into the lab. The recipe card automatically counts **how many crafts the current stock covers** — crafters always see real availability.

## 4. Activate

Flip the recipe live. Then track everything in **Insights**: crafts, crafters, and activity over time.

## Lab presentation

Your lab is your storefront. Customize it (off-chain, changes appear in seconds):

* **Logo** and **banner** (stored on IPFS)
* **Description** and **tags** (Latin only)
* Links: **X**, **Telegram**, **Reddit**, **Website**

## Danger Zone

**Delete Lab** is a *soft delete*: recipes stop accepting deposits and crafts, but **withdrawals stay open** — no one's escrow is ever trapped.

{% hint style="info" %}
Platform limits apply to labs and recipes (shown as e.g. *Labs 3/5, Recipes 1/2* in Explore).
{% endhint %}
