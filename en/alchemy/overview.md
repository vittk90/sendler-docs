# Alchemy Lab — Overview

> **Transmute. Craft. Ascend.**
> Combine your NFTs and tokens into new artifacts through alchemical rituals.

Alchemy Lab is Sendler's on-chain crafting engine. It has two sides:

* **Crafters** pick a live recipe, deposit the inputs, and receive the reward — all in a single wallet approval.
* **Lab owners** create their own laboratory, design recipes, fund the reward stock, and watch the analytics.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Nothing is burned or lost.** Inputs go into **escrow** under a recipe and are always withdrawable from *My items* — even if the recipe or the lab is deleted.
{% endhint %}

## Explore

The **Explore** tab is the pulse of the Lab:

* Platform stats: **Labs**, **Recipes**, **Crafts**, **Crafters**
* **Top Recipes** — the most crafted recipes
* **Top Labs** — the most active laboratories
* **Top Crafters** — leaderboard for *All time* and *This week*

## Labs

The **Labs** tab lists all laboratories with their owner, recipe count, and activity. Search, favorites, and quick sharing included.
