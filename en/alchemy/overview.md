# Alchemy Lab — Overview

> **Transmute NFTs and FTs into new rewards.**

Alchemy Lab is Sendler's on-chain crafting engine. It has two sides:

* **Crafters** pick a live recipe, give up what it asks for, and receive the reward — all in a single wallet approval.
* **Lab owners** create their own laboratory, design recipes, stock the rewards, and watch the analytics.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Nothing is burned or lost.** If you deposit items into a recipe ahead of time, they're always withdrawable from *My items* — even if the recipe or the lab is later deleted.
{% endhint %}

## Explore

The **Explore** tab is the pulse of the Lab:

* Platform stats: **Labs**, **Recipes**, **Crafts**, **Crafters**
* **Top Recipes** — the most crafted recipes
* **Top Labs** — the most active laboratories
* **Top Crafters** — leaderboard for *All time* and *This week*

## Labs

The **Labs** tab lists all laboratories with their owner, recipe count, and activity. Search, favorites, and quick sharing included.

## Adam discount tiers

Hold an **Adam** NFT to lower your craft fee automatically — the higher the rarity, the bigger the discount:

| Rarity | Discount |
| --- | --- |
| Genesis | −100% |
| Unique | −90% |
| Legendary | −50% |
| Epic | −40% |
| Rare | −30% |
| Uncommon | −20% |
| Common | −10% |

{% hint style="info" %}
Genesis is a one-of-a-kind item held by the project's founder — every other tier is available through [Adam Mint](../adam-mint.md).
{% endhint %}

The discount is applied automatically the moment you craft — no extra steps.
