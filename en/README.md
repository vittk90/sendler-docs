# Welcome to Sendler

**Sendler** is an all-in-one platform for NFTs and tokens in the NEAR ecosystem. It runs as a **Telegram Mini App** and as a full **web app** at [sendler.xyz](https://sendler.xyz), so you can trade from your phone or manage everything on a big screen.

## What you can do with Sendler

| Pillar | What it gives you |
| --- | --- |
| 🛒 **Market** | Browse 42+ NFT collections, track floor prices, buy instantly or place orders — in NEAR, stablecoins, or community tokens |
| ⚡ **Auctions** | Bid on live auctions or launch your own in minutes |
| 💼 **Portfolio** | Manage, list, and bulk-send your NFTs across collections |
| ⚗️ **Alchemy Lab** | Combine NFTs and tokens through on-chain recipes — or run your own lab |
| 👤 **Adam Mint** | A limited NFT drop of unique Sendler characters |
| 📊 **Explorer** | Wallet lookup, reputation scores, game leaderboards, and FT holder analytics |

## Why Sendler is different

* **Reputation everywhere.** Every wallet has an on-chain reputation score. You see it in your header, on auction lots, and you can even sort marketplace listings by seller reputation — trade with people you can trust.
* **Multi-currency by design.** Listings and recipes work with NEAR, USDT, USDC, and dozens of community tokens (Darai, Grecha, MED, Emerald, and more).
* **Everything on-chain, nothing lost.** Escrowed inputs are always withdrawable. Failed reward deliveries wait safely in Claimable. Storage deposits are refundable.
* **Native to Telegram and NEAR.** Works seamlessly with both `.tg` and `.near` accounts.

> Sendler is not just a marketplace — it's an operating system for the NFT economy on NEAR.
