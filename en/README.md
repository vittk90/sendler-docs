## Why Sendler is different

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="Multi-currency" %}
Listings and recipes work with NEAR, USDT, USDC, and dozens of community tokens (Darai, Grecha, MED, Emerald, and more).

{% tab title="Nothing is ever lost" %}
Escrowed inputs are always withdrawable. Failed reward deliveries wait safely in Claimable. Storage deposits are refundable.
