# Adam Mint — Limited Drop

> **Own a piece of Adam's legacy.**

Each Adam is a unique character with its own story. Minting supports the project and secures your place in its history.

## Tiers

| Tier | Rarity | Price | Supply |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Sendler** | Common — Legendary | **5 Ⓝ** per mint | 2 900 |
| **Unique Sendler** | 1 of 100 | **50 Ⓝ** per mint | 100 |

Live mint counters show current progress for each tier (e.g. `375 / 2 900` and `40 / 100`).

## Craft fee discounts

Adam isn't just a collectible — holding one automatically lowers your **Alchemy Lab craft fee**, based on rarity:

| Rarity | Discount |
| --- | --- |
| Genesis | −100% |
| Unique | −90% |
| Legendary | −50% |
| Epic | −40% |
| Rare | −30% |
| Uncommon | −20% |
| Common | −10% |

{% hint style="info" %}
Genesis is a single, one-of-a-kind item held by the project's founder — everything from Common to Unique is available through minting.
{% endhint %}

## How to mint

1. Open **Adam** in the navigation (or `/mint/adam` on the web).
2. Pick your tier.
3. Hit **I CHOOSE YOU** and confirm the transaction.
