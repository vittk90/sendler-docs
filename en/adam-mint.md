## How to mint

1. Open **Adam** in the navigation (or `/mint/adam` on the web).
2. Pick your tier.
3. Hit **I CHOOSE YOU** and confirm the transaction.

Adam NFTs also plug into the wider ecosystem — for example, Alchemy Lab recipes like *Common Adam Upgrade* use Adam NFTs as crafting inputs.
