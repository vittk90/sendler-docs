# FAQ

## General

**What is Sendler?**
An all-in-one NFT platform for the NEAR ecosystem: marketplace, auctions, on-chain crafting (Alchemy Lab), a limited character drop (Adam), and analytics (reputation, leaderboards, FT holders).

**Where can I use it?**
As a Telegram Mini App or on the web at [sendler.xyz](https://sendler.xyz).

**Which accounts are supported?**
NEAR accounts (`.near`) and Telegram-linked accounts (`.tg`).

## Trading

**What currencies can NFTs be listed in?**
NEAR, USDT, USDC, and dozens of community tokens (Darai, Grecha, MED, Emerald, and more).

**What's the difference between Buy and Place order?**
*Buy* is an instant purchase at the listed price. *Place order* creates your own order for the item.

**How do auction bids work?**
Bids are in NEAR with an automatic **+5% step**. The card shows the minimum valid bid, the countdown, and the full bid history.

## Alchemy Lab

**Do my NFTs get burned when crafting?**
No. Inputs go into **escrow** under the recipe. Escrow is always withdrawable from *My items* — even if the recipe or lab is deleted.

**What if my reward doesn't arrive?**
It waits safely in **My items → Claimable** (e.g. if you weren't registered on the reward token). Pull it out anytime.

**How do I lower the craft fee?**
Hold a tiered discount NFT (*nft-sender*) — up to **−100%**, applied automatically when you craft.

**What does creating a lab cost?**
One on-chain transaction with a **5 Ⓝ** creation fee; the excess is refunded. Storage deposits are refundable too.

## Adam Mint

**How much does a mint cost?**
**5 Ⓝ** for the Sendler tier (Common–Legendary, supply 2 900) and **50 Ⓝ** for Unique Sendler (1 of 100).
