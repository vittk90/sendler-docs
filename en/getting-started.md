# Getting Started

## Platforms

Sendler is available in two forms:

* **Telegram Mini App** — the full experience inside Telegram, with bottom navigation: *Home, Market, Portfolio, More*.
* **Web app** at [sendler.xyz](https://sendler.xyz) — desktop layout with top navigation (*Market, Auctions, Portfolio, Alchemy, AlchemyV2, Adam*) and a built-in **Command Palette** for quick actions.

## The header — your dashboard

The top bar is always with you:

* **Wallet balance** — your current balance in USD; tap to send funds or view history.
* **Reputation** — your reputation score and global rank (e.g. `13.2K · #230`).
* **Account switcher** — switch between connected accounts.

## Home screen

The home screen is the entry point to the whole ecosystem:

* **All collections** — the live list of NFT collections on the platform (42 and growing; new collections are added as needed).
* **Promo banners** — quick access to Alchemy Lab, the Adam limited drop, the NFT market, Duplo leaderboard, and Reputation explorer.
* **Wallet Lookup** — inspect any `account.near` directly from the home page.
* **Explorer cards** — jump into Duplo rating, Reputation rating, or FT Holders.

## Accounts

Sendler works natively with both **NEAR accounts** (`account.near`) and **Telegram-linked accounts** (`account.tg`).
