# Market

A full-featured NFT marketplace with a multi-currency economy.

## Collection page

Each collection page shows:

* **Floor price** (in USD equivalent)
* **Listings** — number of active listings
* **Offers** — number of active offers
* **Orders** button and **history** toggle

## Browsing & filters

* **Trending** — see what's hot across the platform right now.
* **Grouped listings** — identical NFTs stack into a single card showing the count (e.g. `10 NFTs`) and the lowest *From* price. Toggle **Ungroup** to see each listing separately.
* **Off shop** — filter toggle for items outside the official shop.
* **Search** within a collection.
* **Sorting:** Recently listed · Price ↑ · Price ↓ · **Reputation ↑ / ↓** — a Sendler exclusive that sorts listings by the seller's reputation score.
* **Currency filter** — show listings only in the currency you care about.

## Multi-currency listings

Any NFT can be listed in:

* **NEAR**
* **Stablecoins** — USDT, USDC
* **Community tokens** — Darai, Grecha, MED, SBR, Emerald, Yupland, ELSM, Rye, and more

Prices always show a USD estimate next to the token amount.

## Buying

Open an NFT card to see **all available listings** for that item — each with its price, token ID, and the seller's account. From there you can:

* **Buy** — instant purchase at the listed price.
* **Place order** — set your own order for the item.

## Orders

The **Orders** panel supports:

* **Available / My Orders** tabs
* **Public / Private** order visibility

## Market Activity

A live feed of everything happening in a collection: **listings** and **delistings**, with the item, the account, the price, and the date. Filter by event type and date.
