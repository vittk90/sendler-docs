# Auctions

Sell at the peak of demand — or snipe a deal.

## Browsing auctions

The Auctions page shows all auctions on the platform (hundreds at a time) with a live counter of currently active ones.

* **Filters:** All · **Mine** (your lots)
* Each card shows: rarity badge, stack size (×2, ×3…), **starting price** or **current bid**, reputation value of the lot, and a **live countdown**.

## Auction card

Open any auction to see:

* **Starting price** and the automatic **+5% bid step**
* **Countdown timer** (HRS : MIN : SEC)
* **Bid history** — every bid, live
* **Details:** Token ID, Collection, Seller, End date & time, Status
* **Bid input** with the minimum valid bid pre-calculated (e.g. `Min 0.1 NEAR`) and a **Place Bid** button

## Creating an auction

1. Hit **+ Create** on the Auctions page.
2. **Select a collection** from your portfolio (collections with live auctions are marked, favorites and notification bells supported).
3. Pick the NFT, set the starting price and duration.
4. Launch — your lot appears in the feed with a live timer.

Bids are placed in NEAR. When the timer runs out, the highest bid wins.
