# FT Holders

Top-holder analytics for **NEAR ecosystem fungible tokens** — balances, rankings, and export.

## How it works

1. Open **FT Holders** and pick a token from the grid (or search by name).
2. Get the full holder ranking: **balances and ranks** for every wallet.
3. **Export** the data for your own analysis.
