# Explorer Overview

Data you won't find anywhere else — free, on-chain, exportable.

## Wallet Lookup

Enter any **`account.near`** on the home page and hit **View** to inspect a wallet: assets, activity, and standing in the ecosystem.

## The three explorers

| Explorer | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Reputation rating** | Reputation scores for wallets: trust, activity and contribution across the ecosystem |
| **Duplo rating** | Game leaderboard: top players, progress and seasonal results |
| **FT Holders** | Top holders for NEAR ecosystem fungible tokens: balances, rankings and export |
