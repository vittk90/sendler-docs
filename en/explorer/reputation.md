# Reputation Rating

On-chain reputation scores for the whole ecosystem — **8 000+ wallets ranked**.

## The leaderboard

* Every wallet gets a **reputation score** (top wallets hold hundreds of millions of rep).
* Alongside the score, each row shows **progress stats** across ecosystem activities.
* Tabs: **Top** (the main ranking) and **Reputation NFTs**.
* Sort ascending/descending, paginate through the full list.

## Where reputation shows up in Sendler

Reputation isn't a separate silo — it's woven through the whole product:

* **Your header** always shows your score and global rank (e.g. `13.2K · #230`).
* **Market sorting** — sort any collection's listings by *seller* reputation.
* **Auction lots** display the reputation value of the item.

> Trade with wallets you can verify — reputation makes trust visible.
