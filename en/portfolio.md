# Portfolio

All your assets under control.

## Collection view

Your portfolio is organized by collection. For each collection you see:

* **Owned / Listed** tabs with counts
* The collection's contract address (one-tap copy)
* **Orders** panel and operation **history**
* Search, filters, sorting, and grouping tools

## Per-NFT actions

Every NFT card has two quick actions:

* **Send** — transfer to any account
* **List** — put it on the market for sale

## Bulk send — Send NFTs

The flagship portfolio tool:

1. Open **Send NFTs**.
2. Enter the **recipient** (`account.near` or `.tg`).
3. Use the **filter by title** to find items fast.
4. Send items one by one, or select multiple and send them in a single operation.

Works across your whole portfolio — the list shows every NFT you own with its collection icon, title and token ID.
